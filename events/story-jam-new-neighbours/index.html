Story Jam: New Neighbours

19 - 21 September 2022
part of the Netherlands Film Festival

Applications opening soon

What will we do?

On 19-21 September Storytellers United organises Story Jam: New Neighbours, in cooperation with the Netherlands Institute for Sound & Vision and the Nederlands Film Festival. During three days, participants work together in multidisciplinary teams under the guidance of coaches on new intercultural story prototypes. These stories are:

  • Intercultural: developed with storytellers who are new in the Netherlands, about the added value of cultural cross-pollination.
  • Interdisciplinary: told from the various expertises that team members bring together.
  • Innovative: experimental in the application of new technology and media for storytelling.

Why now?

Influenced by migratory flows, including wars, political unrest and climate crises, the Dutch community is becoming increasingly diverse. Story Jam: New Neighbours focuses on new stories from these multicultural communities and the social and economic added value they contain. What can we mean to each other and what are valuable connections and collaborations? How can we celebrate social and cultural richness? What can different backgrounds learn from each other? And how can intercultural stories help?

With this Story Jam, we want to challenge stereotypes and expose commonly held myths, put migrants' voices at the centre of discussions about migration and deliver innovative messages to promote the integration of new Dutch citizens into local communities.

In short

  • Make social impact with stories that connect, at the intersection of storytelling and technology.
  • Collaborate with storytellers from different disciplines and cultural backgrounds.
  • Develop innovative stories under healthy time pressure, with inspiring input and professional guidance.

Take part

Would you like to join? Register before 15 August using the registration form. The selection of participants will be announced no later than 1 September. Participation is free of charge, catering will be provided.
