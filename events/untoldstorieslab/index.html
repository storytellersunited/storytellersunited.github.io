Untold Stories Lab

16 - 23 July 2021
Fully remote online participation

Kaspar AI, RNDR and Storytellers United invite filmmakers, technologists and designers to take a peek behind the scenes of the interactive online video installation Untold Stories.

Over the course of one week, a select group of participants gets exclusive access to an early version of the interface, creates new screenings out of unseen film footage with the help of Artificial Intelligence and helps to steer functionality and design. Together we will investigate the value hidden in film archives and explore new ways of surfacing stories that have never been told before.

Untold Stories Lab will take place fully remotely online 16 - 23 July, with an opening and public show & tell event. The week in between will act as a sandbox, with participants working flexibly on their own time with the Untold Stories interface. On 20 July we'll gather for an intermediary check-in to exchange thoughts and feedback. A honorarium is available for participant involvement.
